---
title: "Create your LiteFarm account and choose a role"
description: "Sign up at app.litefarm.org with your email or Google account, set your profile, choose your farm role, and join or create a farm."
---

LiteFarm is free to use. All you need to get started is an email address or a Google account. After signing up, you will choose your role on the farm and either join an existing farm or create a new one. The whole process takes just a few minutes.

## Sign up

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Email">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
        Open [app.litefarm.org](https://app.litefarm.org) in your browser. On the welcome screen, enter your email address and click **Continue**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Create your account">
        If your email is not yet registered, LiteFarm will prompt you to fill in your account details:

        - **Full name** — your display name on the farm
        - **Password** — must contain an uppercase letter, a digit, a symbol, and be at least 8 characters
        - **Gender** *(optional)* — collected for research; shared only in anonymised form
        - **Birth year** *(optional)* — collected for research; shared only in anonymised form
        - **Language preference** — sets the language for the app interface

        Click **Create Account** when you are done.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and accept the data policy">
        Read LiteFarm's data policy and check the **I Agree** box to confirm your consent. Click **Continue** to proceed.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Google">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to the sign-up page">
        Open [app.litefarm.org](https://app.litefarm.org) and click **Continue with Google**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Authenticate with Google">
        A Google sign-in dialog will appear. Select or enter the Google account you want to use, then grant the requested permissions.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Complete your LiteFarm profile">
        On your first sign-in, LiteFarm will ask for a few additional details — your display name, language preference, and optional demographic fields. These are collected for anonymised sustainability research. Click **Create Account** to continue.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and accept the data policy">
        Read LiteFarm's data policy, check the **I Agree** box, and click **Continue**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Note>
  LiteFarm is a Progressive Web App. After signing up, you can install it on your phone or tablet by tapping **Add to Home Screen** in your browser. This gives you a native-app experience with offline support.
</Note>

## Choose your role

After completing your profile, LiteFarm will ask: **"What is your role on the farm?"**

Your role determines what you can see and do within a farm. Select the option that best describes you.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Farm Owner" icon="house">
    Full access to all farm data, settings, finances, and team management. Choose this if you own or operate the farm.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Farm Manager" icon="clipboard-list">
    Can manage crops, tasks, locations, and team members. Similar to owner access but without some farm-level settings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Farm Worker" icon="person-digging">
    Can view assigned tasks and log work. Cannot access finances or change farm settings. Suitable for employees and seasonal staff.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extension Officer" icon="graduation-cap">
    Advisory access for agricultural extension workers who support multiple farms. Can view farm data but cannot edit farm settings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  You can also indicate whether the farm is **owner operated** on the same screen. This is optional and does not affect your permissions.
</Tip>

## Join an existing farm or create a new one

After selecting your role, LiteFarm will check whether you already have a farm associated with your account.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Create a new farm" icon="plus">
    If you are setting up LiteFarm for the first time, you will be guided through the farm setup flow — adding your farm name, location, and basic details.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Join an existing farm" icon="envelope">
    If a farm owner or manager has already invited you, your account will be linked automatically once you sign in. The farm will appear in your farm list.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  If you were invited by email, use the **same email address** that the invitation was sent to. Signing up with a different address will not connect you to the farm automatically.
</Info>

## Multi-farm support

A single LiteFarm account can belong to multiple farms. You might manage your own farm while also acting as a worker or extension officer on another.

Once you are a member of more than one farm, LiteFarm will show a **Choose your farm** screen after login. Select the farm you want to work in and click **Continue**. You can switch between farms at any time from within the app.

<Note>
  Your role and permissions can differ between farms. For example, you might be an owner on your home farm and a worker on a neighbouring farm you assist with.
</Note>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I change my role after signing up?">
    Only a Farm Owner or Farm Manager can change your role within a farm. If you selected the wrong role during sign-up, ask the farm owner to update it from the **People** settings page.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if my email is already registered?">
    LiteFarm will detect your existing account and ask for your password instead of creating a new one. Use the **Forgot password** link if you cannot remember it.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is LiteFarm free?">
    Yes. LiteFarm is a not-for-profit platform and is free to use. There are no subscription fees or paywalled features.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which languages does LiteFarm support?">
    LiteFarm is available in English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Hindi, Punjabi, and Malayalam. You can set your preferred language during account creation or update it later in your profile settings.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use LiteFarm on my phone?">
    Yes. LiteFarm works in any modern mobile browser. You can also add it to your home screen as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for a full-screen experience and offline access in the field.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
