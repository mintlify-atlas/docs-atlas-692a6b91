Your profile holds the personal information LiteFarm uses to identify you, communicate with you, and display data in your preferred format. You can update any of these details at any time from the **Profile** section of the app.

To open your profile, tap your name or avatar in the navigation menu and select **Profile**, or go to **Settings → Profile**.

---

## Editing your personal details

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    Navigate to **Profile** from the main navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your name">
    Enter your **First name** and **Last name** in the respective fields. Both fields trim leading and trailing whitespace automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your email">
    Enter a new email address in the **Email** field. Your email is used for login, notifications, and certification export delivery.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your phone number">
    Enter your phone number if you want it on record. This field is optional.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your changes">
    Click **Save** to apply your updates.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Profile photo

You can add a profile photo to personalise your account and make it easier for farm team members to identify you in the People list.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Tap your current photo or avatar">
    On the profile page, tap the circular avatar at the top of the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload an image">
    Choose a photo from your device's photo library or take a new one with your camera.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the selection">
    The new photo appears in the avatar preview. Click **Save** to apply the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Unit preferences

LiteFarm can display measurements in either metric or imperial units. This setting affects how areas, distances, weights, and temperatures are shown throughout the app — including on farm maps, task details, and sensor readings.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Metric">
    Areas in hectares (ha), distances in kilometres (km), weights in kilograms (kg), temperatures in degrees Celsius (°C), and rainfall in millimetres (mm).
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Imperial">
    Areas in acres (ac), distances in miles (mi), weights in pounds (lb), temperatures in degrees Fahrenheit (°F), and rainfall in inches (in).
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

Select your preferred system from the **Units** field on your profile page and click **Save**.

<Note>
Unit preferences can also be set at the farm level in **Farm settings**. Your personal preference applies to your own account across all farms you belong to.
</Note>

## Changing your password

If you created your account with an email and password (rather than signing in with Google), you can update your password from the profile page.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the password section">
    Scroll to the **Password** section of your profile page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter a new password">
    Type your new password in the **New password** field. Passwords must meet the minimum complexity requirements shown on screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the password">
    Re-enter the same password in the **Confirm password** field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to update your password. You will remain logged in on your current device.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
If you sign in with a Google account, password management is handled by Google. The password fields are not shown for Google-linked accounts.
</Warning>
