title: 'Farm settings: manage your farm details and team'
description: 'Update your farm name, address, currency, and units; manage your team by inviting or removing members; and configure third-party integrations.'
---

Farm settings is where you configure the details that apply across your entire farm — not just your personal account. Changes you make here affect all members of the farm. Only Farm Owners and Farm Managers have access to farm settings.

Navigate to **Farm settings** from the main navigation menu. The settings are organised into tabs: **Basic profile**, **Market directory**, and **Add-ons** (the Add-ons tab appears only if you have at least one active integration).

---

## Basic profile

The basic profile tab contains your farm's core details.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Farm settings">
    Navigate to **Farm settings** in the main menu. You land on the **Basic profile** tab by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your farm name">
    Edit the **Farm name** field. This name appears throughout the app and on exported certification documents.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update your farm address">
    Enter or update your farm's address. LiteFarm uses your location to calculate nearby price data in Insights and to filter certifier options for your country.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your currency">
    Choose your currency from the dropdown. This is used in the Finances section and on certification exports.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set your measurement units">
    Choose between **Metric** and **Imperial**. This affects how areas, distances, weights, and temperatures are displayed farm-wide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to apply your changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your team

The **People** section (accessible from the main navigation menu under your farm) shows all current team members and lets you invite new ones or adjust existing roles.

### User roles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Farm Owner" icon="crown">
    Full access to all features, settings, and financial data. Can invite and remove any team member and change roles. Automatically assigned to the person who created the farm.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Farm Manager" icon="briefcase">
    Access to most features including tasks, crops, finances, and certifications. Can invite Farm Workers and Extension Officers. Cannot change the Farm Owner.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Farm Worker" icon="shovel">
    Can view tasks assigned to them, mark tasks as complete, and rate their mood after completing a task. Cannot access finances, insights, or farm settings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Extension Officer" icon="graduation-cap">
    Read-only access to farm data. Extension Officers support farmers in an advisory role and can view records without making changes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Inviting a new team member

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open People">
    Navigate to **People** from the main menu, or go to the **People** tab inside your farm profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Invite user">
    Click the **Invite user** button to open the invitation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter their details">
    Provide the person's first name, last name, and email address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a role">
    Select the appropriate role: Farm Manager, Farm Worker, or Extension Officer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set a wage (optional)">
    For Farm Workers, you can enter an hourly wage. This is used in the labour cost calculations in Finances.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send the invitation">
    Click **Invite**. The person receives an email with a link to create their LiteFarm account and join your farm.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
If the person you are inviting does not yet have a LiteFarm account, they will be prompted to create one when they accept the invitation. If they already have an account, they are added directly to your farm.
</Info>

### Changing a team member's role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the team member">
    Go to **People** and tap the team member whose role you want to change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a new role">
    On the edit screen, choose a new role from the **Role** dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Click **Save** to apply the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Removing a team member

To remove someone from your farm, open their profile in **People** and click **Revoke access**. The person loses access immediately but their historical task and log data is retained. You can reactivate a deactivated user at any time from the same screen.

<Warning>
Revoking access does not delete the user's LiteFarm account. It only removes their association with your farm. If you later want to add them back, you can reactivate them from the People list.
</Warning>

## Add-ons

The **Add-ons** tab appears in Farm settings once you have connected at least one third-party integration. Currently, LiteFarm supports the **Ensemble Scientific (ESCI)** sensor integration.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="View your connection" icon="plug">
    The Add-ons tab shows which integrations are active and displays the organisation ID associated with each connection.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Disconnect an integration" icon="plug-2">
    Click **Disconnect** next to an integration to remove it from your farm. If no integrations remain active, the Add-ons tab is removed from Farm settings.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

To add a new sensor integration, see [Sensors](/features/sensors).

## Market directory

The market directory tab lets you record the markets, buyers, and sales channels your farm works with. This information can be referenced when logging sales in the Finances section.

<Note>
Entries in the market directory are specific to your farm and are not shared with other LiteFarm farms.
</Note>
