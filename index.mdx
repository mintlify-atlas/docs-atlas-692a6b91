LiteFarm is the world's first community-led, not-for-profit digital farm management platform. Built alongside farmers, researchers, and scientists, it gives you the tools to manage your entire operation — from field mapping and crop planning to finances and organic certification — while contributing to participatory research on sustainable agriculture.

## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create your account">
    Visit [app.litefarm.org](https://app.litefarm.org) and sign up with your email address or Google account. LiteFarm is free to use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your farm">
    Add your farm details, draw your field boundaries on the map, and invite your team members to join.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start managing">
    Add your crops, plan your tasks, and begin recording the data that keeps your farm running smoothly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  LiteFarm is a Progressive Web App (PWA), so you can install it on your phone or tablet for offline access in the field. It supports English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, Hindi, and more.
</Note>

## About LiteFarm

LiteFarm was developed at the [UBC Centre for Sustainable Food Systems](https://ubcfarm.ubc.ca/litefarm/) with farmers at the center of the design process. It is open-source software licensed under the [GPL-3.0 license](https://github.com/LiteFarmOrg/LiteFarm/blob/master/LICENSE) and deployed in Canada, the USA, and Latin America.
