LiteFarm is the world's first community-led, not-for-profit digital platform designed to help diversified farmers manage their operations while contributing data to sustainable agriculture research. Built by farmers, researchers, designers, and developers working together, LiteFarm gives you the tools to run your farm efficiently and responsibly — for free.

## Why LiteFarm?

LiteFarm was developed at the UBC Centre for Sustainable Food Systems with farmers placed at the center of every design decision. The platform is purpose-built for diversified farming — operations that grow multiple crops, raise animals, and use sustainable practices.

LiteFarm covers the full lifecycle of farm management across eight core areas:

## Designed for the field

LiteFarm is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — install it on your phone or tablet and use it offline when you're out in the field. It supports ten languages including English, Spanish, Portuguese, French, German, and Hindi, and works with both metric and imperial units.

<Note>
  LiteFarm is deployed in Canada, the USA, and Latin America. If you're a researcher or organization interested in deploying LiteFarm in your region, contact [community@litefarm.org](mailto:community@litefarm.org).
</Note>

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a free account">
    Visit [app.litefarm.org](https://app.litefarm.org) and sign up with your email or Google account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your farm">
    Enter your farm name and location, then draw your fields and other farm areas on the map.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your crops and tasks">
    Browse the crop catalogue, create management plans, and start scheduling work for your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
